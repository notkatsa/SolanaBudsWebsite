Launch - Minting

4200 SolBuds are going to be released onto the Solana blockchain. We will be having a Whitelist sale exclusively for @Bud List members. After our Public Launch we will get listed on Marketplaces and create a special community for our holders.

The DAO

The DAO aka our community wallet is going to be something that only our SolBud holders are going to have access to it. With 50% of the royalties being funded into it each month, our holders are going to be able to choose what to do with the money via voting.

Other utilities
